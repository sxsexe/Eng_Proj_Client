n.
1.
火车；列车 a railway/railroad engine pulling a number of coaches/cars or trucks, taking people and goods from one place to another
to get on/off a train

2.
列队行进的人（或动物）；队列；行列 a number of people or animals moving in a line

[ususing]
一系列相关的事情（或行动） a series of events or actions that are connected

4.
拖裾，裙裾（长礼服的曳地部分） the part of a long formal dress that spreads out on the floor behind the person wearing it
